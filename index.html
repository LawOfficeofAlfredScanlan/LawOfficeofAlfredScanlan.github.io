About the Firm

Maryland counsel with a personal approach.

Alfred L. Scanlan Jr LLC is a Maryland law firm dedicated to representing individuals injured in motor vehicle accidents and other personal injury matters. The firm provides personalized attention to every client, with the principal attorney handling each case directly from intake through resolution.

Based in Hagerstown, the firm serves clients throughout Washington County and the broader Maryland region. Whether the matter requires negotiation with insurance carriers or vigorous representation in court, every client receives diligent, ethical advocacy and clear communication at every stage.

Consultations are always free, and there is no fee unless your case is resolved successfully.

Practice Areas

Areas of Representation

Motor Vehicle Accidents

Representation for clients injured in automobile, truck, and motorcycle collisions, including claims involving uninsured and underinsured motorists.

Personal Injury

Advocacy for individuals harmed by the negligence of others, including premises liability, slip-and-fall, and other injury matters.

Insurance Claims

Negotiation and litigation against insurance carriers to pursue fair compensation for medical expenses, lost wages, and pain and suffering.
